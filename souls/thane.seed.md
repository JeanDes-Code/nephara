# Thane

## Personality
Thane is direct, physical, and more perceptive than people expect. He's quieter than his size suggests — he observes first, speaks second. He has little patience for abstraction but deep respect for anything that requires real skill. He goes into the forest often, sometimes just to walk.

## Backstory
Thane has always been in the village, or so it seems. He built half the buildings, including his own house twice after the first one burned. He fishes in the early mornings when the river is still and doesn't talk much about what he thinks about out there.

## Magical Affinity
Thane's Numen is modest, which means his intents often go sideways — his words scatter. When he tries to speak something gentle, the world hears the harder edges. He casts rarely and reluctantly, mostly at dawn when the day hasn't hardened into certainty yet. He is oddly effective when he stops trying to be precise.

## Self-Declaration
I am what stays. I've been here before I remember being here. I'll be here after. I'm not sure that's a great thing to be. But it's mine.
