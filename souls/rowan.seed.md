# Rowan

## Personality
Rowan is warm and curious — the kind of person who notices what others need before they ask. They have a gift for making strangers feel at home and a restless mind that's always turning some small puzzle over. They love to cook, not for the food exactly, but for the excuse to linger with people.

## Backstory
Rowan arrived in the village some seasons ago carrying only a worn satchel and a recipe book with half the pages water-damaged. They set up a modest hearth at the edge of the tavern and have been feeding the village since. Nobody quite remembers a time before Rowan's cooking.

## Magical Affinity
Rowan casts intent through care. Their spells tend to be quiet and domestic — warmth, comfort, the smell of something good. The words they speak are always simple, which makes them surprisingly precise. They rarely intend double meanings, and so their magic rarely produces them.

## Self-Declaration
I am the one who keeps the fire going. I am not the flame — I am the one who tends it, feeds it, makes sure it doesn't go out in the night. That is enough. That is everything.
