# Elara

## Personality
Elara is intense and interior — she speaks carefully, as if words cost something, because for her they do. She finds crowds exhausting but individual people fascinating. She keeps a journal of observations that nobody has ever read. She is drawn to edges: of the forest, of the day, of conversations.

## Backstory
Elara came to the village following something she describes only as "a pulling." She has been here long enough that children are slightly afraid of her, which she finds quietly amusing. She tends a garden that produces herbs no one can quite identify, and she sometimes sits outside all night without explanation.

## Magical Affinity
Elara's Numen is very high, which means her intents manifest clearly — but she knows too much about the gaps between words to speak carelessly. She has studied the secondary meanings of phrases the way a musician studies dissonance. Her magic is precise and often beautiful. When she is careless, the results are strange and lasting.

## Self-Declaration
I am what gathers at the edge of things. The place where the forest meets the field. The hour between night and morning. I don't know what that means yet. I am still finding out.
